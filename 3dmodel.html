3D Model


The Minecraft Hello Kitty sculpture was the blue print for this 3D Print Project. I started off with a tiny rough draft to see what could be added or what needed to be removed. I had another test print to see if it needed more supports, and then came up with my final. I decided to keep the face flat so it would be easier to paint.
