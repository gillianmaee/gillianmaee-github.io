Minecraft


I decided to choose Hello Kitty for the Minecraft Project. I believe that Hello Kitty is a good fit for cultural impact because she is an icon in pop culture. Her and many other Sanrio characters have been a part of my childhood, and I'm sure for many other girls around the world. She was designed without a mouth because "she speaks from the heart".
